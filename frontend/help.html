Help Center

Answers to the most common questions about using Casuya.

I can't log in — what should I check?

Confirm your email and password (minimum 6 characters). If your account was deactivated, contact your school administrator. You can also use the "Log out" button on your dashboard and sign in again to refresh your session.

Which account types can I register?

Sign-up is available as Student or Teacher. Admin accounts are provisioned by your school and are not self-service. Parents and schools access the platform through the student and teacher experience.

Why am I redirected after clicking a portal link?

Each portal (Admin, Teacher, Student) is role-protected. If you land on a portal that doesn't match your account role, you are automatically sent to the portal for your role, or to login if you aren't signed in. This is expected behaviour.

Can I study offline?

Yes. Casuya is offline-first: published lessons, quizzes, and progress sync locally and continue working without a connection, then sync when you're back online.

Where do I report a bug or request a feature?

Use the Contact Us page or open an issue on our GitHub organization.
