Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026. This is a template policy and should be reviewed by legal counsel before launch.

Information We Collect

We collect the account information you provide (email, name, role, and optional phone number), along with learning activity such as lesson views, quiz results, bookmarks, and progress. Offline usage is cached locally on your device and synced when you reconnect.

How We Use Information

Information is used to authenticate you, personalize your portal, track learning progress, and operate platform features such as analytics, notifications, and payments. We do not sell personal data.

Data Sharing

We share data only with service providers needed to run the platform (for example, payment processors and messaging providers you choose). Schools may access data for their own students and teachers as part of their administration.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data through your school administrator or via the Contact Us page.

Security

Passwords are hashed; access tokens are signed and short-lived. Offline-cached data is stored locally on your device.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be directed to our Contact Us page.
